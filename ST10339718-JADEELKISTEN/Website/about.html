About Us

Our Non-Profit Orphanage was founded in 2012 with a mission to provide care and support for orphaned children. We strive to create a safe and nurturing environment for our children, and we rely on the generosity of our supporters to make this possible.

Our History

Since our founding in 2012, Give Back Orphanage has helped hundreds of orphaned children in need. Our programs have expanded to include education and skills training, health and wellness, and community outreach. We are proud of our accomplishments, but there is still much work to be done. With your support, we can continue to make a difference in the lives of children in need.
